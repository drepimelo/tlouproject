Season One

The Last of Us Season 1 follows Joel, a smuggler burdened by personal loss, as he’s given a mission to escort Ellie, a 14-year-old girl who is mysteriously immune to a deadly fungal infection. Set in a world devastated by a global outbreak, the series takes viewers through a haunting journey across a ruined America, where survival is brutal and trust is rare. As Joel and Ellie travel through abandoned cities, dangerous quarantine zones, and hostile factions, their relationship evolves from reluctant partnership to a powerful emotional bond. But with Ellie’s immunity being the key to a possible cure, Joel is forced to make a choice that could change the fate of humanity — or destroy everything he has left.

Season Two

Five years after their journey across a broken America, Joel and Ellie are trying to rebuild a sense of normalcy in a world that no longer remembers what that means. But peace is short-lived. As shadows of the past return and new threats rise from both the infected and the living, Ellie is forced down a violent path driven by grief, vengeance, and the haunting cost of survival. In a world where trust is rare and every choice leaves a scar, the line between right and wrong fades — and Ellie must confront who she is, and what she’s willing to become.
